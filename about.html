    • Origins

    H-Thai-Ml started out as a food truck catering to the first friday crowd in historic Downtown Las Vegas. After a successful festival run the owner launched his first location in Henderson Nevada on the aptly named HTML lane. Aaron always had a passion for coding, but many didn't know he had a flare for cuisine. Inspired by his 6 month stay in Thailand, aaron ventured into the unknown, creating Vegas' first Southwestern Thai Fusion spot. And thus H-Thai-ML was born!
